About

I'm a multidisciplinary creator working across canvas, page, and screen.

I paint original landscapes of Georgian Bay and the Ontario region, write near-future fiction, and design and ship full-stack web apps — increasingly with AI in the loop. My recent work spans an online painting gallery, Baylight: After Image (a story told as book, audiobook, and microdrama), and a solo-built World Cup bracket-pool app.

Whether the medium is oil, prose, or code, the throughline is the same: make the thing end to end, and sweat the craft.
